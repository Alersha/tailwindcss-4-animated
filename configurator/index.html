The Timing properties define when, how fast and how often the animation is executed. Duration and Delay support values from 100 to 1000 in increments of a hundred.

The easing property specifies the speed curve of the transition effect.

The direction property sets whether an animation should play forward, backward, or alternate back and forth between playing the sequence forward and backward.

The Fillmode sets how the animation applies styles to its target before and after its execution.
